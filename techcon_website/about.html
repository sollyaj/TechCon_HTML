Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts and has since grown into a global event attracting thousands of professionals, developers, and innovators each year. From its humble beginnings, TechCon has become a launching pad for major product announcements and thought leadership in the tech industry.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and drive technological advancement across industries. We believe in empowering communities through knowledge sharing, inclusive practices, and future-focused conversations.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ayesha Khan

Dr. Khan is a pioneer in ethical AI development. Her keynote in 2022 addressed AI governance and transparency, setting the tone for global discussions on responsible technology.

Marcus Liu

Founder of QuantumOS, Marcus shared groundbreaking ideas on post-silicon computing and the future of quantum architecture.

Elena Rivera

As a cybersecurity expert and advocate for open-source security, Elena emphasized the importance of transparency in digital infrastructure.
